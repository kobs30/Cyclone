#COT Cyclone Owner Token

Cyclone Owner Token confirms your ownership of a share in the Cyclone Blockchain network’s Validator license.


The supply of COT does not increase over time, this means that COT is a most valuable and highly speculative asset of the Cyclone Layer1. COT can be traded on exchanges and you can manage it as you see fit.


#COT has two simultaneous sources of profit: First, its own value growth over time, and second, a percentage of all network emission in native CYCL coins (income from all network transaction fees) that holders regularly receive.
